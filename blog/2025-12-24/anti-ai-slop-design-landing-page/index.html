Some ideas to make your website design not looking like every other generated AI page

1. Steal like an artist

  • Try prompt "100 award winning website design"
  • Take 1 part of the website you find pleasing to your eyes
  • Replicate it, prompt it to the exact details, the font, the exact color, the animation
  • IMPROVE it, this is the important step, you want to differentiate, be different, how to stand out? how to make it yours, how to be yourself.

2. Steal like corporate

  • Go to top 500 fortune company that is related to niche you want (Nike, Wise, Airbnb)
  • They already have top designer designing their website
  • Take what you feel like working, make your own twist
  • Have bunch bunch bunch 100, 1000 references
  • The devil is in the details
  • Make a product on the site
  • See if people actually convert
  • Not working? Just change a little bit and see if it converts, repeat

3. How to prompt

  • "Make a metaprompt to have design like Apple website"
  • "What's the name of this drawing, design choice, this art" - ask everywhere what is this, prompt that
  • The devil is in the details (again)
  • Don't ask AI a lot of thing at once, do one thing at a time, sharp corner, glassmorphism, neobrutalist design, skeuomorphism, whatever, one thing at a time
  • Why does this not like what my imagination, imagine better, see a demo video or live website site by site using second screen
